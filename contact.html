Frequently Asked Questions

What is the A.R.T.S?

The A.R.T.S is a digital skills training program designed to empower you with the latest digital skills, including web development, digital marketing, cybersecurity, Data Analysis, Graphicd Design, UI/UX Design and Computer Literacy.

Who can apply for the scholarship program?

The scholarship program is open to anyone who are interested in acquiring digital skills and who are willing to commit to the five-month training program.

What is the duration of the scholarship program?

The scholarship program lasts for five months.

What are the courses offered in the program?

The program offers courses such as Web Development, Digital Marketing, UI/UX Design, Cybersecurity, Graphics Design, Digital Literacy, and Office Productivity.

How much is the program fee?

The program fee is 300,000 naira, but the scholarship partners have subsidized the price to 50,000 naira for students.

Can I apply for the program online?

Yes, you can apply for the program online by click register and filling the application form on this page.

When is the application deadline for the program?

The application deadline for the program is May 12th, 2023.

What is the mode of training for the program?

You can choose your preferred training mode, which can be physical (onsite) from one of our training centers or virtual (online) from our e-learning platform.
